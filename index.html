VARIETIES

Amber

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

McIntosh

Real McIntosh apples from Uttarakhand, UP, and Himachal Pradesh have a sweet-tart taste, red-green skin, off-white flesh, and are used in desserts and eaten raw.

Suneheri

Sunehari apples, a hybrid of Ambri and Golden Delicious, have yellow peels with crimson streaks, and are crunchy, juicy, with a sweet-acetous taste.

Maharaej

A large apple with red and green color. It tastes a bit sour but sweetens with time and is available by late October.

Chaubattia

Chaubattia apples, a hybrid of Early Shanburry and Red Delicious, are sweet-tangy with glossy red stripes, grown in Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand.

Golden Delicious

A variety with comparatively longer shelf life, it is crispy, juicy and has thick greenish-white flesh which turns golden upon ripening. It is available till January.
